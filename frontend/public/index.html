What EnglishUp does

Practice-first, score everything

EnglishUp is a practice platform. It gives you realistic prompts for speaking and writing, scores your performance on clear, defined metrics, and keeps a history of your results so you can see your progress over time.

There are no lessons, curricula, or personalised learning paths — just deliberate practice with honest feedback.
